Frequently Asked Questions

This is a shortlist of our most frequently asked questions. For more information about Today, or if you need support, please create an issue from here.

What is Todo App?

Todo App is a Todo List-keeping web app where you can add, delete, search, and mark tasks as completed. It provides a convenient way to manage your tasks and stay organized. The best part is, once you visit the site, you can use it offline without an internet connection.

Do I need to register to use Todo App?

No, you don't need to register anywhere to use Todo App. It's completely hassle-free. Simply visit the site and start managing your tasks right away.

Is Todo App privacy-friendly?

Absolutely! Todo App respects your privacy and ensures that all your data is stored locally on your device. It never leaves your device, providing you with peace of mind regarding the security and privacy of your information.

Do I need to install Todo App to use it?

No, you can use Todo App directly on the website without installing anything. However, for a more optimized and enhanced experience, we recommend installing it as a Progressive Web App (PWA) on your device. This allows you to access Todo App quickly and easily, just like a native app.

Do I need an internet connection to use Todo App?

No, Todo App is designed to work offline as a Progressive Web App (PWA). You can use it seamlessly even without an internet connection. This feature ensures that you can manage your tasks anytime, anywhere, without worrying about internet connectivity.

Is Todo App available on multiple devices?

Absolutely! Todo App is cross-platform compatible and works on various devices, including iOS, Android, Windows, macOS, and Linux. Your tasks will be synchronized across all your devices, allowing you to access and manage them seamlessly from any device.
